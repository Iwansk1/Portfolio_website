About me!

My name is Iwan Bijl I am 22 years old and live in the Netherlands.
Currently I am working on becoming a front-end web developer.
before I even started with front-end development I was trying to become a 3D-artist, but after I finished that study I was not very fond of the idea of that being my fulltime job.
So I decided to do another study this was CMD (Communication and Multimedia Desgin, I am still busy with this study) During this study I discovered front-end development and have been working on learning and improving this ever since.
Apart from my studies I am very passionate about cars and especially autocross(the european version).


3D art

During my time as a 3D-artist I was mostly working on hard surface modeling.
For the most part I was busy making weapons, this was also influenced by the internship I did.
I did my internship at the ministry of defence at a military base in Amersfoort.
Ever since I started CMD the amount of time I spent working on 3D models has drasticlly decreased, but I am trying to pick it up again.


Development

At the start of my CMD study I did not know what to do, so I did a little bit of everything: Video editing, 2d animations, illustration and some UI/UX design.
The UI/UX design is what pulled me towards web design at first but then I tried out some coding and found this way more rewarding then making some pretty wireframes, so I started to shift my focus from UI/UX desgin to front-end development, and now I am here creating my own portfolio website.
